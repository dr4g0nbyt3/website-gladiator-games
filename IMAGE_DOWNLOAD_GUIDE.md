# Non-Steam Game Images - Download Guide

This guide will help you download official images for games not available on Steam.

## Directory Structure
All downloaded images should be placed in: `/public/images/games/non-steam/`

Images should be in landscape format (preferably 460x215 or 16:9 ratio) and saved as PNG or JPG.

---

## Games to Download

### 1. **Valorant**
**Source:** https://www.riotgames.com/en/press
- Download: "VALORANT Logos" ZIP (271.17kB)
- Or visit: https://playvalorant.com/en-us/media/
- Save as: `valorant.jpg` or `valorant.png`
- Look for: Key art or logo on dark background

### 2. **League of Legends**
**Source:** https://www.riotgames.com/en/press
- Download: "League of Legends Logo" PNG (5.88MB)
- Or visit: https://www.leagueoflegends.com/en-us/news/media/
- Save as: `league-of-legends.jpg` or `league-of-legends.png`
- Look for: Main game logo or key art

### 3. **World of Warcraft**
**Source:** https://blizzard.gamespress.com/world-of-warcraft
- Navigate to "Assets" section
- Download a key art or logo image
- Save as: `world-of-warcraft.jpg` or `world-of-warcraft.png`

### 4. **Diablo III**
**Source:** https://blizzard.gamespress.com/Diablo-IV
- Look for Diablo III materials in the press center
- Or check: https://blizzard.gamespress.com/Diablo-III-BlizzCon-Press-Kit
- Save as: `diablo-3.jpg` or `diablo-3.png`

### 5. **Minecraft**
**Source:** Official Minecraft assets
- Visit: https://www.minecraft.net/en-us/usage-guidelines
- Or search for official Minecraft logo/key art
- Save as: `minecraft.jpg` or `minecraft.png`
- **Note:** You already have `/public/images/games/minecraft.webp` - verify if you want to use that or download a new one

### 6. **Escape from Tarkov**
**Source:** https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/146tDercIJZaPY8mVNWWJX0be0mI-CuLl
- Open the Google Drive press kit
- Download a key art or logo image
- Save as: `escape-from-tarkov.jpg` or `escape-from-tarkov.png`

### 7. **Marvel Rivals**
**Source:** https://www.igdb.com/games/marvel-rivals/presskit
- Download key art from the press kit page
- Or visit: https://www.marvel.com/games/marvel-rivals
- Save as: `marvel-rivals.jpg` or `marvel-rivals.png`

### 8. **Roblox**
**Source:** https://corp.roblox.com/press-kit
- Download official Roblox logo or key art
- Save as: `roblox.jpg` or `roblox.png`

### 9. **EverQuest** (if needed)
**Source:** Search for official Daybreak Games press materials
- Save as: `everquest.jpg` or `everquest.png`

### 10. **EverQuest 2** (if needed)
**Source:** Search for official Daybreak Games press materials
- Save as: `everquest-2.jpg` or `everquest.png`

### 11. **Ultima Online** (if needed)
**Source:** Search for official Electronic Arts or Broadsword Online Games press materials
- Save as: `ultima-online.jpg` or `ultima-online.png`
